INDUSTRIES WE SERVE

Powering Critical Global Sectors

SVC precision-engineered flow control valves are trusted across major sectors to manage high pressure, extreme temperature, and aggressive media safely and reliably.

Water & Wastewater

Managing municipal water distribution, desalination plants, and industrial sewage lines with bubble-tight resilient seated designs that prevent contamination and leakage.

Oil, Gas & Chemical

Withstanding corrosive chemicals, hazardous gases, and aggressive media in refinery processes using double-offset and triple-offset fire-safe valve designs.

Power Generation

Regulating high-temperature cooling circuits, steam cycles, and thermal systems in coal, gas, and hydro power stations with frictionless metal-to-metal seating.

HVAC & Fire Safety

Providing reliable, fast-acting flow controls for commercial HVAC systems, high-rise buildings, chilled water networks, and high-performance fire protection and control valves.

Marine & Offshore

Securing offshore drilling rigs, cargo tankers, and ballast water management networks with salt-water resistant bronze-aluminum and high-alloy valve body coatings.

Heavy Manufacturing

Sustaining high-intensity industrial processing lines, steel mill furnace circuits, pulp & paper systems, and automotive assembly cooling setups.
