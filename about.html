Satguru Valve Corporation (SVC) is a premier manufacturer of high-precision industrial flow control solutions, engineering safety and reliability for critical global sectors.

OUR FOUNDING

How It Started: Our Story

Founded in 1995 in Ahmedabad, Gujarat, Satguru Valve Corporation began with a humble dream: to manufacture top-tier, zero-leakage industrial valves right here in India. Our founder envisioned a local manufacturing unit that would combine precise engineering with honest, hands-on client relationships.

Instead of taking a standard corporate approach, we focused heavily on building a skilled team of local artisans and design engineers. In the early days, we worked closely with local factories, refining disc geometries and rubber seal compounds to solve pressure drops in aggressive municipal water channels.

Over the decades, that small workshop grew into a state-of-the-art manufacturing facility. Today, SVC is trusted by India's largest infrastructure leaders. Throughout our growth journey, we have kept our approach human-centric—ensuring that every engineer, partner, and customer receives personal attention and adaptive engineering support.

Our Mission

Absolute Safety & Reliability

To design, manufacture, and supply high-precision, bubble-tight industrial valves that ensure absolute system safety and zero fluid loss. We strive to empower critical industries with robust, long-lasting flow control components, engineered locally and manufactured to global quality standards.

Our Vision

Global Engineering Benchmark

To stand as the global benchmark for fluid regulation technology. We aim to expand our engineering horizons into complex metallurgy—such as high-alloy duplex steels—and advance sustainable manufacturing processes that protect environment resources while powering national infrastructure development.
